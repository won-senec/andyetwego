Say hello

The world doesn’t pause for any of us — and yet we go. I’d rather move through it in good company than alone, and that’s really what this place is for.

I write here as a scientist, a husband, and a new father — usually late at night, after the house has gone quiet. Some of these entries were written in emptier rooms, back when I was living alone. These are the things I keep coming back to across the seasons of my life, and I share them in the hope that they meet you somewhere.

So if something here moved you, unsettled you, or made you want to argue back, tell me. Share what you’re thinking, making, or watching. Tell me where I’m wrong. Or just say hello.

Every message reaches me directly, and I answer each one myself.
